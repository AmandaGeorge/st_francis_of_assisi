Staff

St. Francis of Assisi Parish is a family. As disciples of Jesus, we are “children of God” and “brothers and sisters in Christ.” Jesus himself says, “Whoever does the will of my Father in heaven is my brother, and sister, and mother” (Matthew 12:50). We are here to serve you in your faith journey.

Sacraments

The liturgy and the Eucharist are at the heart of our Catholic faith. We offer weekly and holiday liturgies and reconciliation. If you need information on the sacraments of baptism, first holy communion, confirmation, marriage, holy orders, or anointing of the sick, we are here to help you.

Community

Our parish is a vibrant and diverse community of Catholics and seekers devoted to ongoing personal and communal growth and conversion to Jesus Christ. We warmly invite all to come as you are and join us for an event and see how God is calling you to belong to his family.
